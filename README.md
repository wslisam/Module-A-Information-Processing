This web app is designed to help students easily understand how computers (and humans!) handle data in the modern world. 

## 🎯 How to Use This App for Learning

This app is divided into 5 easy-to-follow sections. For the best learning experience, we recommend going through them in order:

1. **System Components (系統組件):** 
   Start here! Click on the 5 different icons to discover what makes up an Information System. *Hint: It's not just computers!* Read the "Supermarket Example" to see how they work together in real life.
   
2. **Information Processes (資訊處理過程):** 
   Explore the 7 steps of processing. Click on each step in the flow diagram to reveal both computer-based and everyday human examples. Pay attention to the "FAQ" box to clear up common misunderstandings!
   
3. **Data vs Information (數據與資訊):** 
   This is the most important concept! Use the **Baking Analogy** to visualize the difference. Remember: Data is like raw eggs, Information is the baked cake!
   
4. **Information Age (資訊時代):** 
   Hover over or tap the **DIKW Pyramid** from bottom to top. Watch how raw data slowly transforms into human wisdom. This section will explain why "Information Literacy" is your ultimate survival skill today.
   
5. **Knowledge Check (知識測驗):** 
   Ready to test yourself? Take the 5-question interactive quiz. Don't worry if you get something wrong—every answer comes with a detailed explanation to help you learn from your mistakes.

## 💡 Tips for Self-Study
* **Use the Language Toggle:** Stuck on a difficult English word? Click the "中文" button at the top right anytime to switch the entire page to Traditional Chinese.
* **Read the "Think & Link" Boxes:** At the bottom of each section, these boxes will help you connect what you just learned to the next topic. They are great for quick reviews!
